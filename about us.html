About Us

Fresh Creps is a local store located in Grove Street, Los Santos which opened in 1200 BC. We book shoes for customers who can't come directly to our store that day. We are also open to shipping shoes (if within Los Santos). At Fresh Creps we have all kinds of shoes, from Tokyo, all the way to Los Santos. We are soon planning to renovate our shop, which is why everything in the store is on sale for 50% off! If you want to return a product or have any problems occur please visit our contact us page.
